# CR,Assy (Reference No.508)

Adjustment /

When replacing/removing this part, refer the following pages and make sure to perform the specified operations including required adjustment.

  • Repair Parts Adjustment/Checks Items and Order
1. Removing the Housing,Upper Front,Sub

  1. Open the Printer Cover.
  2. Remove the two screws (A) securing the Housing,Upper Front,Sub (B).
  3. Slightly lift the side with the screw holes of the Housing,Upper Front,Sub (B) and release the two dowels , and then remove the Housing,Upper Front,Sub (B) in the direction of the arrow.

    • : + No.2 / 3x10 P-tite (BK)

2. Removing the Cover,CR

  1. Rotate the Roller,PF,Assy (A) in the direction of the arrow and release the CR Lock.
  2. Move the CR,Assy (A) to the position shown below.
  3. Remove the two screws (A) securing the Cover,CR (B).

    • : + No.1 / 2.5x8 P-tite (BK)
  4. Slightly lift the Cover,CR (A) and release the two dowels , and then slide the Cover,CR (A) in the direction of the arrow to remove the two hooks , and remove the Cover,CR (A).


    Check Point /

    When disassembling or assembling the Cover,CR (A), take care not to damage the two hooks of the Cover,CR (A).

3. Performing the Ink eject (Adapter Assy)

  1. See "Minimizing Ink Leakage during Disassembly for Your Safety" and flush the ink out of the Adapter Assy.

    Check Point /

    Perform the following work after setting the Cam Lever,Valve to the closed state (Lever on the front side).

4. Removing the Adapter Assy

  1. Disengage the Sheet,Guide Tube (A) from the three hooks  on the Case,Valve (B).
  2. Disengage the Sheet,Guide Tube (A) from the two hooks  on the Case,Valve (B).
  3. Remove the two screws (A).

    • : + No.2 / 3x10 P-tite
  4. Remove the six screws securing the Pressing Plate,Adapter (B).

    • : + No.1 / 2.5x8 P-tite
  5. Slide all the Pressing Plate,Adapter (A) toward the front side of the printer respectively, and release the Pressing Plate,Adapter (A) from the hole (B) on the Print Head, and then remove all the Adapter Assy (C) and all the Pressing Plate,Adapter (A).


    Assembly /

    When installing the Adapter Assy (A) and Pressing Plate,Adapter (B) on the Print Head (C), while pressing the Adapter Assy (A) from the top, slide the Pressing Plate,Adapter (B) and place the Pressing Plate,Adapter (B) in the hole (D) on the Print Head.

5. Removing the Seal,Adapter

  1. Remove the six Seal,Adapter (A).


    Assembly /

    Note that the Seal,Adapter (A) must be installed in a certain direction.
    Install the Seal,Adapter (A) so that the notch on the upper part is on the right side and the notch on the lower part is on the left side.

    • Front: The photo should be directed to the adapter side.
    • Back: The photo should be directed to the Print Head side.

6. Removing the Print Head

  1. Remove the four screws securing the Print Head (A).

    Check Point /

    When assembling or reassembling the Print Head, move the CR,Assy (B) to the position shown below.

    • : + No.1 / 2.5x8 P-tite
    Assembly /
    • When tightening the screws to secure the Print Head, do not use a electric screwdriver. Tighten the screws with hands.
    • Tighten the screws in the order as shown above.
  2. Lift the Print Head (A) and disconnect the FFC,Head, Assy (B) from the connectors of the Print Head (A), and remove the Print Head (A).


7. Removing the Cover,Tank

  1. Open the Printer Cover.
  2. Lift it in the direction of the arrow while the Cover,Tank (A) is closed and disengage the two hooks (B).
  3. Remove the Cover,Tank (A) by sliding it in the direction of the arrow.

8. Removing the Cover,Top,Assy

  1. Open the Cover,Printer,Assy (A) and remove the screws.
    • L8050 Series: two screws
    • ET-18100 Series/L18050 Series: four screws
    • : + No.2 / 3x10 P-tite (BK)
  2. Open the Cover,ASF (A) and remove the two screws.

    • : + No.2 / 3x10 P-tite (BK)
  3. Slide the Cover,Top,Assy (A) in the direction of the arrow and disengage the hooks .
  4. Lift the Cover,Top,Assy (A) in the direction of the arrow to remove it.

9. Removing the Housing,Left

  1. Remove the two screws on the top of the printer.
    • :  + No.2 / 3x10 P-tite (BK) 
    • :  + No.2 / 3x10 P-tite
  2. Remove the screw on the back of the printer.
    • :  + No.2 / 3x10 P-tite (BK)
  3. Insert a flathead screwdriver into the position shown in the figure and disengage the hook (B) of the Housing,Left (A).
  4. While disengaging the hooks (B), slide the Housing,Left (A) in the direction of the arrow and remove it.


    • : Hooks

10. Removing the Housing,Right,Assy

  1. Remove the screw on the top of the printer.
    • : + No.2 / 3x10 P-tite
  2. Remove the screw on the back of the printer.
    • : + No.2 / 3x10 P-tite
  3. Remove the screw.
    • : + No.1 / 2.5x8 S-tite (BK)
  4. Warp the protrusion (B) on the Housing,Right,Assy (A) outward to release it.
  5. While releasing the protrusion (B), slide the Housing,Right,Assy (A) in the direction of the arrow and remove it.

11. Removing the Housing,Upper Rear

  1. Remove the four screws, and then remove the Housing,Upper Rear (A).

    • : + No.2 / 3x10 P-tite (BK)

12. Removing the Scale,CR

  1. Remove the Extension Spring,Scale CR (A).
  2. Disengage the Scale,CR (B) from the protrusion of the Frame Main,Assy (A) and remove it.


    Assembly /

    During installation, be sure that the black mark on the Scale,CR (A) is positioned on top on the full-digit side.

13. Removing the Holder,Pully Driven,Assy

  1. Remove the Spring,Belt CR (A).

  2. Remove the Holder,Pully Driven,Assy (A) by sliding it in the direction of the arrow.

14. Removing the Motor,CR

  1. Disconnect the Motor,CR Cable (CN30) (B) from the Main Board (A).
  2. Release the Motor,CR Cable (B) from the Frame Base Left (A).
  3. Release the Motor,CR Cable (B) from the Frame Main,Assy (A).
  4. Release the Motor,CR Cable (B) from the Frame Base Right (A).
  5. Remove the two screws and remove the Motor,CR.

    • : + No.2 / 3x6 S-tite Tapping

    Assembly /
    • When screwing the Motor,CR (A) onto the Frame Main,Assy, install the Motor,CR (A) in orientation shown in the figure.
    • When routing the Motor,CR Cable (A), bundle it with the Cable,PS,Assy (C) using acetate tape and place the bundled part at the hooks of the Frame Main,Assy (B).



15. Removing the Housing,Upper Front

  1. Remove the four screws.
    • L8050 Series
    • ET-18100Series/L18050 Series
    • : + No.2 / 3x10 P-tite (BK)
  2. Remove the Housing,Upper Front.L8050 Series

    • Hold the rear of the Housing,Upper Front (A), lift it in the direction of the arrow, and remove it.
    • ET-18100 Series/L18050 Series
      Disengage the five hooks  of the Housing,Upper Front (A), lift it in the direction of the arrow, and remove it.

    Assembly /

    Follow the procedure below to assemble.

    1. Insert the front part of the Housing,Upper Front (A) into the Housing,Front,Left (B).
      At this time, check that the hooks of the Housing,Front,Left (B) is engaged with the Housing,Upper Front (A).
    2. Install the Housing,Upper Front (A) by pressing its rear.

16. Removing the Housing,Front,Assy

  1. Disconnect the FFC,Panel (B) from the Panel,Assy (A).
  2. Release the FFC,Panel (B) from the Housing,Front,Assy (C).
  3. Disconnect the Cable,Board,Wi-Fi (B) from the Board,Wi-Fi (A).
  4. Remove the screw.

    • : + No.2 / 3x10 P-tite
  5. Remove the two screws.

    • : + No.2 / 3x10 P-tite
  6. Disengage the hooks  shown in the figure using a flathead screwdriver.
    • L8050 Series: one hook
    • ET-18100 Series/L18050 Series: three hooks

  7. While lifting the part of the Housing,Front,Assy (A) shown in the figure upward, move it in the direction of the arrow to disengage from the protrusion of the Frame,Front,Assy (B).

  8. Remove the Housing,Front,Assy (A) by sliding it in the direction of the arrow.

    Assembly /

    Follow the procedure below to assemble.

    1. Set the two screw holes (B) (C) on the right side of the Housing,Front,Assy (A) into the printer.
    2. While lifting the part of the Housing,Front,Assy (A) shown in the figure upward, move it in the direction of the arrow and set it into the Frame,Front,Assy (B).
    3. Push the Housing,Front,Assy (A) in the direction of the arrow and install the Housing,Front,Assy (A).

17. Removing the Key Slot,Assy

  1. Remove the four screws, and remove the Key Slot,Assy (A).

    • : + No.2 / 3x10 P-tite (BK)
    • : + No.1 / 2.5x8 S-tite

18. Removing the Holder,Cam,Assy

  1. Remove the screw.

    • : + No.1 / 2.5x10 P-tite 
  2. Disengage the hook  of the Holder,Cam,Assy (B) from the Frame Main,Assy (A).
  3. Disengage the dowel  of the Holder,Cam,Assy (B) from the Frame Main,Assy (A) and remove the Holder,Cam,Assy (B).

19. Removing the IS Assy

  1. Rotate the Roller,PF,Assy (A) in the direction of the arrow and release the CR Lock, and then move the CR,Assy (B) to the edge of the full-digit side.
  2. Remove the two screws, and remove the Frame Base Right Support (A).

    • : + No.2 / 3x12 P-tite
    Caution /

    When installing the Frame Base Right Support (A), be sure that the screw hole of the Frame Base Right Support (A) is inside the screw hole of the Base,Right Support (B).

  3. Remove the screw securing the Joint,MTB (B), and release the Waste Ink Tube (C) from the groove (D) on the Guide,Tube Supply and the Frame,Front,Assy.

  4. Remove the three screws, and remove the IS Assy in the direction of the arrow.

    • : + No.2 / 3x10 P-tite 
    Assembly /

    When installing the IS Assy, tighten the screws in the following order.

20. Removing the Holder,LD

  1. Remove the two screws.
    • : + No.2 / 3x10 P-tite Washer
  2. Disconnect the Cable Detector,LD,Phase (B) from the Detector,LD,Phase (A) and remove the Holder,LD (C).

21. Removing the Slider,Trans

  1. Remove the Spring,Slider Trans (A).
  2. Remove the Slider,Trans (A) by sliding it in the direction of the arrow.

22. Removing the CR,Assy

  1. Remove the screw and remove the Holder,Scale CR (A).
    • : + No.2 / 3x6 S-tite 
  2. Remove the two screws securing the Support,Frame Main (A).

    • : + No.2 / 3x10 P-tite 
    • : + No.2 / 3x6 S-tite
  3. Lift the Support,Frame Main (A) in the direction of the arrow and remove it.
  4. Rotate the Roller,PF,Assy (A) in the direction of the arrow and release CR Lock, and then move the CR,Assy (B) to the edge of the Full side.
  5. Disengage the two hooks, and remove the Lock,Tube (A).
  6. Release the Tube,Adapter,S/L (C) from the Guide,Tube Supply (B).
  7. Remove the two screws, and remove the Guide,Tube Supply (B).

    • : + No.2 / 3x6 S-tite 
    • : + No.2 / 3x10 P-tite
  8. Remove the screw.

    • : + No.2 / 3x12 P-tite
  9. Disengage the dowels  and remove the Base,Right Support (A) by sliding it in the direction of the arrow.
  10. Slide the CR,Assy (A) in the direction of the arrow and remove it from the Frame Main,Assy (B).


    Assembly /
    • Insert the section (C) of the Frame Main,Assy (D) into the slit (A) of the CR,Assy (B).
    • Insert a flathead screwdriver between the Slider,APG Rear (B) and CR,Assy (A). create a clearance, and assemble the parts.
    • [ET-18100 Series/L18050 Series only] When installing the CR,Assy and routing the FFC,Head, Assy (A) and FFC,Detector PW (B), pass the FFC,Head, Assy (A) and FFC,Detector PW (B) through the Sheet,Guide Head FFC (C) as shown in the figure.
