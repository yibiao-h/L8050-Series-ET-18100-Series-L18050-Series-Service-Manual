# PG Type

This product has five types of PGs. The following describes the details of PGs. The PG depends on the relative position of the carriage to the Lever,APG. The PG set once is held until the next PG shift operation.

Check Point /

  • Implement PG adjustments in the PG - state.

  • Printer enters PG Typ. or PG - with a normal power off. (differs by paper setting)
